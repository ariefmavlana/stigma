About STIgma

STIgma is a place for long-form writing that respects your intelligence. No algorithmic noise. No engagement bait. Just ideas, explored carefully.

This publication is powered by Django and CrewAI — a trio of AI agents (Researcher, Writer, Editor) that can assist in drafting posts which are then reviewed and published by human editors.

Every AI-assisted post is clearly marked with the ✦ AI-Assisted badge. We believe in transparency: AI helps us research and draft at speed, but every published word has been reviewed by a human.

The Stack

  • Backend — Python 3.12, Django, PostgreSQL 17
  • AI — CrewAI with specialist agents for research, writing, and editing
  • Frontend — TailwindCSS, editorial monochrome design
  • Package management — uv

Philosophy

The name STIgma is deliberate. Before digital publishing, the stigma was where ideas transformed into permanence. We hold to that standard: every post should be worth the ink.
