About
STIgma

STIgma is a place for long-form writing that respects your intelligence. No algorithmic noise. No engagement bait. Just ideas, explored carefully.

The Platform

This publication is powered by Django and CrewAI. It utilizes a trio of sophisticated AI agents (Researcher, Writer, Editor) that assist in drafting comprehensive deep-dives.

Every AI-assisted post is clearly marked with the ✦ AI badge. Transparency is non-negotiable: while AI accelerates coverage, every published word is curated and verified by human editors.

The Stack

  • Framework: Python 3.12, Django 5.x
  • Database: PostgreSQL 17
  • Intelligence: CrewAI (Llama Models / OpenAI)
  • Design: STIgma Monochrome Engine, Tailwind
